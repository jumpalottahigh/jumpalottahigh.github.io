Ultimate Stalker

/web app/

A bootstrap mobile web app using youtube, facebook and twitch APIs. Implementation time - less than 8 day. Usage - enter a name. Press 'Stalk!' Track the person on the specific service. Your feed gets automatically updated every 1 minute.

Launch
Story Time TL;DR
Currently in the second semester of the second year we have a mobile web app course in school. The project assignmet as follows: utilise client side technology and 3rd party open APIs to make a mobile (responsive/fluid design) app. The scope is up to us to decide. I am working with James Narraway on it and we have decided to develop "Ultimate Stalker". What our app will do is basically allow you to get info on a favorite person of yours you are following on a number of services - Facebook, Twitter, Twitch, Youtube. So that whenever the person releases new content or goes live, you will know immediately from your app, regardless of the service they are using

Rollin' Shark

/android game/

Rollin' Shark started as a project developed from scratch during the Microsoft DevShark Event in Helsinki on 14th Dec 2014.

Download file_download
Story Time TL;DR Slides Video

We started by brainstorming ideas, game design, UI design and shortly after moved on to developing the game in Unity. Assets and code alike were created on the spot.

As stressful as the event was, it was also definitely exciting to try and make a game in 24 hours. The outcome, somewhat satisfying and definitely promising, will be worked on in the near future and soon released on Google Play.
